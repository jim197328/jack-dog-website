ABOUT ME

Get In Touch

Hi, I'm Tracey Plimmer and the pooch at the end of the pier is Jack!

I have provided a caring and professional dog walking service for dogs living in Over and offer one-to-one (or sometimes buddy walks with two dogs that are best friends) treating them as if they were my own since 2019. Whether it's energetic field walks, gentle senior strolls, or popping in to care for puppies, I'm here to give your pet the attention they deserve.

Having used a dog walker whilst I was in full time employment, I know first hand how important and how reassuring it is to have a trustworthy, reliable and fun carer looking after and giving your furry family member a safe, happy and stimulating time if you are unable to.  So rest assured that your pet will receive the encouragement and attention they deserve.

I live in the village and hope that I have met and chatted to lots of fellow dog owners during mine and Jack's walks and cycles through the fields:

  • I am DBS checked and cleared
  • Hold Public Liability insurance
  • Have completed a canine first aid course
